Common Exercises

Burpee

Burpees are a great, full-body exercise to increase your strength and endurance. Start standing, drop into a squat, kick your feet back, return to squat, then jump up.

Plank

Planks build your core strength. Get in a push-up position, rest your forearms on the floor, and hold as long as you can.

Mountain Climber

Mountain climbers are a good cardio exercise. From a push-up position, alternate bringing knees toward your chest as quickly as you can.
